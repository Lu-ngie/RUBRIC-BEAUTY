Skin peels and facials

A facial peel, also known as a chemical peel,
is a liquid applied to your face. It sits on your skin for several minutes, working to removing the top layer.
It’s called a peel because that top layer of your skin will peel off following the treatment — usually within the next few days.

OUR BODY SALTS AND SCRUBS

Body scrubs are a mechanical exfoliant,
meaning they physically remove dead skin cells from the outer layer of your skin with abrasive ingredients,
like sugar or salt. This stimulates skin cell turnover, resulting in smoother,
brighter skin and possibly preventing future acne breakouts.
